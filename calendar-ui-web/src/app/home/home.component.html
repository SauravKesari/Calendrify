About Calendrify

Enables seamless scheduling, notifications, and team collaboration for efficient time management.

Effortlessly schedule and manage your meetings and events with our intuitive Calendrify. Stay organized, receive notifications, and collaborate seamlessly with your Group.
Our calendar app allows you to effectively manage your schedule by providing a user-friendly interface for adding and organizing meetings. Once you log in, you can easily schedule and track your upcoming meetings, ensuring you stay on top of your commitments.
Additionally, our app offers a notification system that sends reminders for your scheduled meetings, ensuring you never miss an important event. These notifications can be customized based on your preferences, helping you stay organized and punctual.
